A custom cake — designed with you

Tell us the date, the size, the flavour, and a few notes. We confirm the spec back to you, and the team starts baking once it's approved.

What we'll ask

  1. Size and tiers — small (1.0 kg) / medium (1.5 kg) / large (2.5 kg). Up to three tiers.
  2. Flavour — start from one of our classics or describe a flavour profile.
  3. Filling — custard, cream, fruit, nut.
  4. Decoration — kept simple. Inscription is included.
  5. Pickup or delivery — pickup at 350 Promenade Way, or delivery in our zones.
  6. Date and time — 48 hours minimum lead time.

Start the conversation

Open the chat in the corner and write what you have in mind. The assistant pulls the catalog, lead-times, and delivery zones live from our system and drafts a spec sheet. We confirm the order before we bake.
